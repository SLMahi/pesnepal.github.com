About Us

PES,Popular Environment Service works in green sector. We help companies lower all the environmental impacts and works in different area such as forest management plan, design, EIA/IEE in different sectors.

Our Vision

“To work on and boost the sustainable development of the nation by protecting and preserving the environment.”

Our Objective

prepare the necessary documentation and prepare business preparation and proposal of the environmental issues and ensure that the client gets all the assistance necessary for the formulation and implementation of the suggested environmental solution.

Our CSR

"Work closely in collaboration with local stakeholders and district agencies to identify environment impacts and its mitigation activities."

PES Expertise

Following proper guidelines mentioned under "Environmental Act" of Nepal , PES Team provides high quality, technically sound EIA and IEE study reports on wide areas.

Technical Support

PES Team is highly capable of providing support on different topics such as Environmental Legislation, Policy, Research Studies, Consultation and Environment Protection technically or generally.

Forest Management

We are competent in formulating various forest management techniques like timber extraction and consumption, forest fire control, afforestation and forestry development.

DDR Management

Disaster Risk Management is quite an essential prerequisite for a project and we are able to assist in formulating such plan and policies including solid-waste management and urban-hazard management

Plan/Policy Formulation

Our Team Members are actively participating in national and international programes regarding plan and policy formulation related to environment and environmental factors.

Solutions for projects

Promoting sustainable development project is one of our objective. So, we are continously exchanging a wide solutions for complications in a project related to either social or environmental factors.

Popular Environmental Service

Popular Environment service (PES) works in green sector of Nepal. It was established on 26 day of September,2017(2074/06/10) which is located in Shantinagar,Kathmandu (Contact office Jawagal, Kupondole).

It helps companies lower all the environmental impacts and works in different area such as Forest Management, Soil Conservation, Pollution, Environmental research, Environmental and Forestry related Training and other services. We provide experts assessment and advisory services for our clients regarding environmental issues. We make sure that revised environmental policies comply with environmental laws, regulation and deliver awareness on the importance of Conservation, Management, Sustainability of all environmental related matters.

About us

Pes Nepal is a up-growing environment consultant on EIA, IEE in Nepal based on New Baneshwor, Kathmandu Nepal. Our main activities include proper planning, feasibility studies, survey, supervision and management of the broad projects primarily related to social and environmental sciences.

Our team is intentionally small, eclectic, and skilled; with our in-house expertise, we provide sharp and specific outcomes understanding the current trends.
